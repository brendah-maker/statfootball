Bournemouth Stun Manchester City: 1-0 Victory Hands Title to Arsenal

SF

StatFootball News Desk

May 19, 2026 • 6 min read

In a night of pure footballing theater at the Vitality Stadium, AFC Bournemouth delivered the knockout blow to Manchester City’s title defense.

The Cherries' 1-0 victory didn't just secure three points for Andoni Iraola’s men; it officially confirmed **Arsenal as the 2025/26 Premier League Champions**, ending the Gunners' 22-year wait for domestic glory.

Advertisement

The Kroupi Moment: City’s Title Hopes Vanish

The decisive moment came in the 40th minute. Adrien Truffert surged down the left wing, carving through the Citizens' backline before sliding a perfectly weighted pass to **Junior Kroupi**. The 19-year-old sensation turned Mateo Kovacic with ease and curled a sublime finish past Gianluigi Donnarumma into the far post.

Arsenal: The Champions of England

As the final whistle blew in Bournemouth, the party began in North London. Arsenal’s 1-0 win over Burnley 24 hours earlier had put the pressure on City, but few expected the Premier League standings to be settled this way. With City now five points behind the Gunners with only one match remaining, Mikel Arteta’s side has officially clinched the trophy.
